Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Bhakti365 ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Location Data: With your permission, we collect your device's location to provide accurate Panchanga calculations and nearby temple information.

Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for app functionality and analytics.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the App, including features accessed and time spent.

Preferences: Your theme preferences, notification settings, and other customization choices are stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
• Provide accurate Panchanga calculations based on your location
• Display nearby temples and spiritual places
• Send notifications based on your preferences
• Improve app functionality and user experience
• Analyze app usage patterns to enhance features
• Provide customer support

3. Data Storage and Security

Your preferences and settings are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. Location data is used in real-time for calculations and is not permanently stored on our servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services for:
• Analytics and performance monitoring
• Location services
• Content delivery

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

5. Location Permissions

The App requests location permissions to:
• Calculate accurate Panchanga based on your geographical location
• Find nearby temples and spiritual places

You can control location permissions through your device settings. Denying location access may limit certain features of the App.

6. Notifications

With your consent, we send notifications for:
• Daily Panchanga updates
• Festival wishes and reminders
• Daily shlokas
• Important announcements

You can manage notification preferences in the App's Settings or through your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide the App's services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. You can delete your locally stored data by uninstalling the App or clearing app data through device settings.
